About EDUBench

EDUBench is a global education platform dedicated to empowering students from over 120 countries. While AP courses offer students in certain regions the opportunity to earn additional college credits and boost their GPA, EDUBench provides an equivalent academic advantage—accessible to all students, regardless of location.
Our programs are designed to deliver high-quality, globally recognized certifications in collaboration with some of the world’s most prestigious universities. We offer expertly crafted content taught by renowned tutors from across the globe, ensuring students receive top-tier instruction. To maintain academic integrity and global credibility, all exams are administered in official exam centers located within each participating country.
1 With EDUBench, every student has the opportunity to stand out in college applications and advance confidently toward their academic and professional goals.


Mission Statement

At EDUBench, our mission is to democratize access to advanced academic opportunities for students worldwide. We aim to provide high-quality, globally recognized learning experiences—equivalent to AP courses—that help students enhance their academic profiles, earn college credit, and unlock opportunities regardless of their location or background. Through partnerships with leading universities, we strive to empower the next generation of learners with the tools and credentials they need to succeed.
